Orders & Shipping

Payments & Coupons

We currently offer Cash on Delivery (COD) — pay when your order arrives at your doorstep. No advance payment or card details required. Online payment options are coming soon.

On the Checkout page, you'll find a coupon section on the right side. Enter your coupon code and click Apply. The discount will be reflected immediately in your order total before you confirm.

GST (18%) is calculated and displayed at checkout, after any discounts are applied. Your invoice includes a detailed GST breakup for record keeping.

Account & Returns

You need a free account to place orders, track shipments, and use the wishlist. Registration is quick — just your name, email, and a password.
